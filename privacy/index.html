Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Gus Digital Solutions LLC ("we", "us", or "our") built ProfitCalc as a free application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

Information We Do Not Collect

ProfitCalc is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • We do not collect usage analytics or behavioral data
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

All product data, cost calculations, and margin reports you create within ProfitCalc are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

No Account Required

ProfitCalc does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the app.

Third-Party Services

ProfitCalc does not integrate with or send data to any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

ProfitCalc does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
